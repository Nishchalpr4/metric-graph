# 📊 HOW TO GET DETAILED DRIVER ANALYSIS

## 🎯 THE CORE ISSUE

**Why "Explain revenue for Company_380" doesn't show drivers:**

Revenue is a **BASE METRIC** from SEC filings. Companies report the final revenue number directly - they don't break down:
- How many customers/orders
- What was the average order value  
- What was the pricing strategy
- Marketing effectiveness

This detailed transactional data doesn't exist in SEC filings - only the final revenue number.
